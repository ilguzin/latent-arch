Google released Gemini 3.6 Flash, 3.5 Flash-Lite, and a specialized 3.5 Flash Cyber security model, while notably omitting a Gemini 3.5 Pro, prompting questions about the company's flagship strategy. Flash Cyber is positioned as a cheaper alternative to Anthropic's Mythos for finding and patching vulnerabilities.
