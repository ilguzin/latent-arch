AMD committed up to $5 billion to Anthropic, which will deploy up to 2 gigawatts of AMD Instinct MI450 GPUs on the new Helios rack-scale system starting in 2027. The deal marks a significant expansion of AMD's AI infrastructure footprint against Nvidia's dominance.
